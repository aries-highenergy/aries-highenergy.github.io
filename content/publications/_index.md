## 1. High-Impact & Discovery Highlights

*   **Observation of inverse Compton emission from a long γ-ray burst**
    *   *Authors:* MAGIC Collaboration (including **K. Misra**)
    *   *Journal:* **Nature**, 575, 459–463 (2019)
    *   *Brief:* Discovery of sub-TeV gamma-ray emission from GRB 190114C, validating the presence of synchrotron self-Compton processes in relativistic shocks.
    *   *Links:* [[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-019-1754-6)]

*   **An Extremely Luminous Panchromatic Outburst from the Nucleus of a Distant Galaxy**
    *   *Authors:* A. J. Levan, N. R. Tanvir, S. B. Cenko, ... **K. Misra**, et al.
    *   *Journal:* **Science**, 333, 203–206 (2011)
    *   *Brief:* Detailed multi-wavelength analysis of Swift J164449.3+573451, representing a relativistic jet triggered by the tidal disruption of a star by a supermassive black hole.
    *   *Links:* [[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1207143)]
