# Research Areas

## Gamma-Ray Bursts

Studying prompt emission, afterglows, energetics,
and progenitor systems.

## Kilonovae

Investigating neutron star mergers and their
electromagnetic counterparts.

## Host Galaxies

Understanding how host galaxy properties influence
GRB populations.

## Multi-Messenger Astronomy

Connecting electromagnetic observations with
gravitational wave detections.
